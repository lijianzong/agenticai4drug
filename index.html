About Us

Bridging AI & Drug Discovery

Scinnohub is an AI-driven drug discovery platform that combines cutting-edge machine learning with deep expertise in computational chemistry and structural biology.

Our mission is to compress the drug discovery timeline — from target identification to optimized lead — by replacing slow, costly experimental cycles with rapid, accurate in-silico prediction.

Built by interdisciplinary teams spanning medicinal chemistry, bioinformatics, and AI research, our studio provides the tools researchers need to accelerate every stage of the pipeline.
